About

The Artist

A little corner of the world where beauty is found in the quietest moments.

Hello, I'm Nysa, a painter based in India endlessly inspired by the way light filters through petals and the gentle passage of seasons. My work explores tenderness, memory, and the dreamlike quality of everyday beauty.

Each painting begins with a feeling, a color caught at dusk, the curve of a rose before it opens fully. I work primarily in oils and watercolor, letting the medium lead me somewhere softer than words.
